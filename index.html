About me

I’m a life-science generalist with a strong lab background and a growing focus on AI-powered productivity. I like building workflows that turn messy, repetitive tasks into reliable systems. My scientific training taught me to design experiments, analyze data, and communicate clearly—skills I now apply to human-in-the-loop AI tooling.

About the course

An 8-week live cohort on practical GenAI for research and work. Weekly workshops, live help sessions, feedback on assessments, and a coached capstone. Topics range from LLM basics and document creation to data analysis, text analytics, custom GPTs/RAG, automation, and research communication.

Bootcamp features

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Private community & personalized feedback
  • Capstone coaching & shareable certificate
  • Lifetime access to tutorial library

Grading rubric

  • Online quizzes – 15%
  • Workshop assignments – 35%
  • Attendance & participation – 10%
  • Final project – 40% (pass at 80%)

Module 1

Websites, documents & presentations

LLM basics; generating Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; building simple research sites; structured documents; AI-assisted presentations.

Module 2

AI-automated data analysis

Code Interpreter for analysis; dashboards; batch data transformation; automated reports & visualizations; spreadsheet automations.

Module 3

GenAI for research & text analysis

Literature review tools; classification; sentiment; knowledge maps; summarization pipelines; synthesis systems.

Module 4

Custom GPTs & chatbots

Chat architecture, context windows & RAG; custom GPTs; Botpress chatbots; knowledge-base integrations.

Module 5

AI for task automation

Email automations; agent workflows; browser-control agents for web tasks.

Module 6

Communication & outreach

AI-generated summaries; figures & diagrams; video abstracts; prompts for visualization; content scheduling.

Module 7

Capstone & showcase

Project check, peer feedback, final presentation, and public Q&A.
